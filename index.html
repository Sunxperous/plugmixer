Goodbye plug.dj

As plug.dj shuts its doors on 28 September 2015, I would like to thank the users who have supported and made good use of the Plugmixer script and extension over the past year. While there were quite a few bugs and hiccups along the way, I am glad to have received your continued support and valuable feedback, both of which have guided the development process of Plugmixer.

It has been a pleasure to serve you.
